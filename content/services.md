## Specialized Fibre Solutions

### Design and Consulting

Offering tailored network design and consulting services, leveraging over 25 years of experience to ensure optimal performance and cost-effectiveness for projects of any scale.

### Installation and Splicing

Providing expert installation and splicing services for various applications, including data centers, enterprise networks, and long-haul connections.

### Testing and Certification

Utilizing advanced equipment to conduct comprehensive testing services, such as:
- Optical Time-Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) analysis
- Chromatic Dispersion (CD)
- Polarization Mode Dispersion (PMD)
- Attenuation Profile (AP) tests

Ensuring networks meet industry standards and perform at high data rates.

### OTDR Network Audits and Reporting

Leveraging our extensive expertise in fibre network testing, we provide impartial evaluations of various network types, including:
- Enterprise networks
- Long-haul telecommunications
- Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) networks on power lines

Each audit culminates in a comprehensive report outlining our findings and tailored recommendations to enhance your network's efficiency and reliability. Further, we offer analysis of OTDR test results provided by clients, delivering professional insights to ensure your network meets industry standards and performs optimally.

High-quality fibre optic cables are the backbone of reliable networks, ensuring high data rates and long-lasting performance. Our trusted manufacturers offer a wide selection of fibre cables, including:
- Blown fibre cables

MLNetwork provides high-quality fusion splicers, offering both core alignment and cladding alignment machines to suit a variety of applications.
